American Robin

" Robins have about 2,900 feathers "

  • Scientific Name: Turdus migratorius
  • Food Types: Invertebrates
  • Wingspan Average: 31 to 41 cm (12 to 16 in)
  • Population: 370 million

The American Robin is very active in the daytime and rest with his flock at night. A Robin's nest is made up of mostly grass. The nest consist of grass,twigs, paper, and feathers.
